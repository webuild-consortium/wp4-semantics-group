This document describes the European Business Wallet Vocabulary v0.1, i.e., the Vocabulary used by the Verifiable Credentials of the European Business Wallet Framework.

Alternate versions of the vocabulary definition exist in Turtle and JSON-LD.

Published:
2026-06-25
Version Info:
0.1
See Also:https://webuild-consortium.github.io/wp4-semantics-group/ebwv

This vocabulary has been defined by the Semantics goup of the Webuild Consortium and provides the RDFS vocabulary for the Electronic Attestation of Attributes of the European Business Wallet framework. Comments regarding this document are welcome. Please file issues directly on GitHub, or send them to tbd.
